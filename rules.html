Rules:

By sympoietic art collective
September 28, 2024

drafting document for the rules and principles behind this project, and designed to make this project work.

  • To participate, and be listed as a member you must:
    • Contribute some resources and services to the collective's shared artistic commons. These are materials that you agree any member of the collective will be allowed to use without restriction, and services or tools that you offer to make available to other participating artists.
    • Contribute the mimimum expected input (perhaps 1 piece or a few pages of art and some commentary every 4-6 months or so)
    • Acknowledge that this is a participatory project which artists join with no expectation of remuneration. There is also low participatory expectation, but there is a minimum expected input.
    • Agree that any art you make using materials from the commons must be in turn contributed to the collective's commons, and made available to participating artists to do with as they please (draw on for inspiration, copy, modify, remix, etc).
  • If a member chooses to leave the collective:
    • They may decide whether current and future participants can keep using their contributed materials to make art for this project (all or nothing, as it will otherwise become really hard to keep track of if the project gains momentum).
    • If a member leaves and makes their works unavailable as of their departure, they will be asked to contribute new materials to the collective, should they decide to rejoin.

    • The works they have contributed to the commons up ultil then will stay in the collection of the collective's works. The collective will keep a copy of the works, and has the right to display them. Artists otherwise retain rights on their work, including the right to sell them. However, the collective must always have access to a facsimile of contributed work.
